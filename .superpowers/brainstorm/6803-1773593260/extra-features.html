Additional analytics ideas — what resonates?

Beyond the 4 planned charts. Select any that you'd want included. These are things you might not have thought of but could add real value on a cattle farm.

💉

Treatment Withdrawal Tracker

Table of animals currently on medication showing days remaining until withdrawal period ends. Critical for food safety — you'd know exactly which animals can't go to market yet.

🌿

Camp Stocking Rate / Grazing Pressure

Each camp's current headcount vs its size (hectares). Shows which camps are overstocked and which have capacity. Helps make smarter movement decisions.

🐄

Calving Rate Trend

Monthly bar chart of calvings over time. Are calf numbers increasing or declining year-on-year? Quick herd growth indicator.

📉

Deaths & Sales Over Time

Monthly trend of animal losses (deaths + sales). Helps spot abnormal mortality spikes or seasonal patterns.

⏰

Overdue Inspection Alerts

List of camps that haven't been checked in more than 48 hours (based on your FarmSettings threshold). Already have the config in the DB — just need the UI. Good for the Oorsig overview too.

🔀

Animal Movement Flow

Which animals moved between which camps this week/month? Useful for tracking grazing rotation patterns and spotting camps that receive too much pressure.
